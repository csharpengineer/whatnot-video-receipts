# Whatnot Video Receipt

Whatnot Video Receipt transforms your Whatnot shopping experience with three powerful enhancements: watch the exact moment your item sold, get richer details on every order page, and explore your complete purchase history in a powerful filterable grid — all without leaving Whatnot.

**This is available for installation from the Chrome Web Store: ▶️ https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/whatnot-video-receipt/pkhnhhnkccohcebmhmedeakmgfpdmnkd?hl=en-US

▶️ VIDEO RECEIPT PLAYER

Ever wonder what the bidding looked like when you won that item? The Video Receipt feature lets you watch the exact livestream moment your item was sold, right on the order detail page.

When you open any order from a live auction, a "Watch Video Receipt" button appears near the top of the page. Click it and an in-page video player opens directly — no new tabs, no searching through old streams. The video automatically skips to the moment your item came up, so you see the host present the item, the countdown, and the winning bid.

From the player you can jump to the sale moment, download a clip of just the sale window as an MP4, or download the full livestream recording. If an order is older than 60 days or was not from a live auction, the button is grayed out with a clear explanation.

ℹ️ ENHANCED ORDER DETAIL PAGES

Every order detail page gets a set of improvements that surface information Whatnot already has but doesn't always show prominently.

Full timestamps — The order date is shown with the exact time, not just the date.

Return deadline — The "Return By" date is calculated and displayed so you always know how much time you have.

Shipping details — The carrier name and logo appear under the "Track your purchase" section. The tracking status title is shown in bold, with a green "Arriving Today" badge or a red "Delayed" badge when applicable.

Sales channel pill — A small label on the item title tells you whether it was a Live Auction, Fixed Price listing, or Auction.

Seller card — The seller's profile photo appears as a circular avatar next to their name. Premier sellers get the Whatnot wings badge. Verified sellers get a blue checkmark. The seller's full bio and stats (rating, average shipping days, total items sold) are shown below their name.

📃 ACTIVITY FEED ENHANCEMENTS

The purchase history sidebar that appears on any Whatnot page gets additional detail injected into each order row.

Each row is enhanced with the seller's avatar and premier badge, the full timestamp including time of purchase, the shipping carrier and estimated delivery date, and a one-line preview of the item description — all visible without opening the order.

📊 ADVANCED ORDERS GRID

The centerpiece feature is a full-screen, spreadsheet-style orders grid that pulls your complete purchase history and displays it in a powerful, interactive table. Access it by clicking the "Advanced Orders" button that appears in the Whatnot navigation.

The grid loads your complete purchase history and displays every order in a fast, virtual-scrolling table. Even with hundreds or thousands of orders, scrolling stays smooth.

Every row includes the order number as a clickable link, purchase date and time, order status, sales channel, transaction type, item title with thumbnail image preview, item description, category, seller name with avatar and badges, quantity, item price, subtotal, shipping, tax, auth fee, credits applied, total, shipping service name, estimated delivery date, tracking status, and tracking number with a direct carrier link.

Tracking numbers are automatically linked to the right carrier. USPS package numbers link directly to the USPS tracking page. USPS letter and flat mail numbers link to parcelsapp.com, which has access to USPS Informed Visibility data for letter mail that the public USPS tracking page does not show. UPS shipments link directly to the UPS tracking page.

Hover over any item thumbnail to see a large preview popup. Hover over a seller avatar to see their profile photo enlarged. Each row from a live auction has a play button to open the Video Receipt player directly from the grid.

Toggle any column on or off using the column picker. Filter your orders by seller, status, sales channel, transaction type, or category. Click any column header to sort. A sticky footer row shows running totals for all numeric columns based on your active filters. Order data is cached locally for 15 minutes so the grid reopens instantly.

🔒 PRIVACY

This extension has no analytics, no telemetry, and no external servers of its own. It reads data from Whatnot's own APIs using your existing logged-in session and displays it locally in your browser. No order data, personal information, or usage data ever leaves your device or is sent anywhere other than Whatnot's own servers. The extension only activates on whatnot.com and has no effect on any other website.
